About

Come on out to Big Al's Farmers Market to enjoy some of the freshest produce Tuscaloosa has to offer! Our vendors are happy to show off their hard work at each event. We can be found every other Saturday in Tuscaloosa, Alabama.

Tickets for the next event go on sale every Sunday after the previous event. Customers may purchase tickets here on our website through the events page. Vendors may purchase booths through the vendor portal.

Why Big Al's?

The farmer's market allows you to eat with the seasons. Seasonal food is fresher and more nutritious than out of season food.

With the addition of BigAl's Farmers Market, customers can personally meet the growers of the food they are consuming. Here you can see the care the farmers put in growing the food for the community. It allows you to boost your local economy and keep it running. Our local vendors love serving you, and what better way to support them than to give back directly to them instead of a large chain of grocery stores.
